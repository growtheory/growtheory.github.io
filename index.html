About our services

Content Creation

Every digital strategy starts with content. If you don't have consistent relevant content for your marketing campaigns and social media pages it will be impossible to grow your audience. At Grow Theory we understand that producing high quality content takes work and resources. Our content creation service will help you bridge the gap between no engagement and high engagement without having to dedicate a whole team to content production. Whether it's words, images or full blown videos you can count on us to capture the true essence of your brand and get the word out.

Social Media Management

We firmly believe that social media is the future of digital engagement and growing audiences. Managing social accounts is no longer a job for interns to do in their spare time, it is a full time job that requires dedicated resources if you want to take full advantage of existing and future social platforms. Our social media management services takes the burden off your team, allowing them to focus on business critical issues and leaving the social strategy to specialists. We will manage the day to day running of your social media accounts and engage with your audience so that you can focus on what matters most to your business.

Paid Media

Paid media is the process of purchasing digital advertising on various platforms such as Google Adwords or Facebook Ads. Running a digital ad campaign requires a large time investment and deep knowledge of the current advertising landscape on your chosen platform. If you do not have the time or knowledge to dedicate to perfecting and optimizing your campaign you are likely leaving a significant amount of money on the table. Partner with Grow Theory to achieve maximum results while reducing overspending on your advertising campaigns.

Search Engine Optimization

The art of search engine optimization or SEO has been around for over a decade now. SEO refers to optimizing webpages for optimal placement in organic search results. While it takes longer to build up a solid and stable organic search campaign, the results can pay dividends for years to come by driving consistent targeted traffic to your website without having to buy ads. We believe that there really is no generic SEO strategy for every client. At Grow Theory we tailor our SEO techniques to suit your specific business and vertical. By using tested methods that don't try to "game the system" like other agencies, we protect your business from getting hit by regular algorithm changes.

Conversion Rate Optimization

While most agencies focus on driving traffic to your website, Grow Theory specializes in what happens after your visitors get to your site. Whether you are looking to drive up sales or to increase your userbase, conversion rate optimization or CRO is crucial to turning clicks into relevant key performance indicators (KPIs) for your company. We use special tools and statistics to analyze all relevant data points throughout your application. After we gather enough information we can suggest and implement solutions to help you reach your goals more quickly without having to spend more money on advertising.

Cloud Distribution

Once your application is receiving a significant amount of traffic, scaling it up to meet the demands of your audience can be a tricky task. At Grow Theory we assist our clients in moving their applications to the cloud in a seamless and efficient manner to minimize down time so that you can continue growing your business. Along with distributing applications to cloud services and providers, we also provide in depth monitoring and analytics solutions so you can spot performance issues or other bottlenecks before your users do.
